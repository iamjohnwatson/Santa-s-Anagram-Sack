How to Play

Drag letters into the slots to form the hidden holiday word.

  • Submit a correct word for +100 points.
  • Wrong submissions cost 25 points and break your streak.
  • No shake bonus: +50 when you solve without shaking.
  • Streak bonuses: 3 (+25), 5 (+50), 10 (+100).
  • Shakes reshuffle unplaced letters only.
